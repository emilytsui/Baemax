A Huggable and Hand-Shaking Desktop Baymax!

Our Desktop Baymax has two laser-cut acryllic arms that are attached to servos.

The servos are powered by an Arduino Uno, located in the back of Baymax.

Everything is pieced together with a plush doll suit.





When one of two buttons is pressed, Baymax will either hug or give a handshake.
