About Baymax

Baymax is an inflatable robot from Disneyâ€™s animated film Big Hero 6, inspired by robot arm made of balloons. Soft robotics, robots built with materials such as fabric and balloons, allows advantages such as lower weight and cost compared to the conventional metal robots. Soft robots also allow for safer and more portable applications. The purpose of our project is to build our own soft robot whose actions include basic interactions such as hugging, handshaking, and fist bumping.
