our plan

Our plan is to repeatedly build and test. We will purchase one robotic arm kit at first, chosen with consideration of all of our specifications. While we wait for the arm to arrive, we will design a metal frame to be placed inside the skin. After building the arm, we will complete tests to make sure it can imitate a hugging motion. During this process, we will also be working on making the metal frame. We will then mount the arm on the frame inside the skin and complete more tests.
